Company Themes - The 3 Universal Findings
1. The Coaching Gap11 / 15 teams (73%)
Leaders doing too much operational work instead of working through their teams. Teams want trust and empowerment. Not enough strategic work happening because leaders are consumed by tasks their teams should own.
2. Prioritisation13 / 15 teams (87%)
When everything is a priority, nothing is a priority. Teams are too reactive, not proactive enough. They struggle to push back and ask for clarity. Not enough time for structured, strategic work.
3. Meeting Culture10 / 15 teams (67%)
Too many meetings. Too many meetings people do not need to be in. No consideration when booking meetings. No time to do other work because the calendar is full.
